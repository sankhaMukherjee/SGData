Information on the data:

The following files should be downloaded into this folder:

 1. resale-flat-prices-based-on-approval-date-2000-feb-2012.csv
 2. resale-flat-prices-based-on-registration-date-from-march-2012-onwards.csv

Both these files may be obtained from the following site: https://data.gov.sg/dataset/resale-flat-prices

There is no need for any web scrapping at the moment. Just download the files in this folder and you will be good.
